🕕 Early Morning

5–6 soaked almonds + 3–4 soaked raisins
1 glass warm water with ghee or lemon

🍽️ Breakfast (8–9 AM)

1 paratha with ghee + sabji, 1 bowl curd or 50g paneer, 1 fruit (papaya, apple or chikoo)

🍵 Mid-Morning Snack (11 AM)

Banana + milk + peanut butter shake (add oats for extra)

🍛 Lunch (1–2 PM)

3 rotis, 1 bowl rice, dal, sabji, curd/raita, sprouts or besan chilla

🫖 Evening Snack (5–6 PM)

Roasted chana + trail mix + tea/milk

🍽️ Dinner (8–9 PM)

Choose meals with protein: dal chilla + curd, khichdi, jowar roti + sabji, or veggie pav bhaji

🛌 Before Bed (9:30–10 PM)

Haldi doodh + soaked dates or walnuts

🥜(Bonus) Vegetarian Protein Boosters

  • Paneer (100g): 18g
  • Moong sprouts (1 cup): 14g
  • Besan (100g): 22g
  • Milk (1 cup): 8g
  • Greek yogurt: 10–12g
  • Peanut butter (2 tbsp): 8g
